Treasury Liquidity & Cash Budgeting Tool
📌 Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive liquidity budgeting framework designed to manage and forecast cash flows for a residential real estate portfolio. By modeling monthly income, renovation costs, debt service, and operating expenses, this tool enables proactive financial planning and liquidity risk assessment.

The system utilizes Excel-based sensitivity and scenario analysis to assist management in making informed decisions regarding capital injections, renovation timelines, and rental strategies.

📊 Analytical Components
The model provides a robust analysis of financial performance under varying operational conditions:

1. Scenario Planning
The Scenario Summary allows users to stress-test the portfolio against positive, realistic, and pessimistic outlooks. By adjusting variables like rent levels, renovation costs, and duration, the model calculates the necessary external capital injection required to maintain solvency.
![Scenario Summary](Plot2.png)

2. Liquidity Forecasting
The liquidity budget visualization tracks the portfolio’s cash position over a 12-month horizon. It clearly differentiates between the baseline liquidity and the capital injections required to bridge projected shortfalls during the renovation phase.

3. Sensitivity Analysis
To understand the impact of strategic decisions, this tool evaluates how fluctuations in rent and maintenance costs (such as refrigerator replacements) affect cash reserves across each month of the fiscal year.

4. Risk Mitigation & Impact Assessment
Beyond the baseline forecasts, the system evaluates secondary risks, such as rental delays and variable renovation durations. This ensures that the treasury team has a clear view of the potential capital burden under varied timelines.

Renovation Efficiency: The model highlights the direct financial impact of reducing renovation periods from 4 months to 2 months.

Operational Risk: By modeling rental delays, the system helps quantify the "buffer" required to handle market-related occupancy risks.

🚀 Key Functionalities
Cash Flow Projection: 12-month forecasting of net liquidity positions.

Automated Scenario Modeling: Dynamic adjustment of inputs to instantly view funding requirements.

Sensitivity & Break-Even Analysis: Identifying the thresholds where external financing becomes unavoidable.

Executive Reporting: Automated PDF generation for stakeholder review.

🛠️ Technical Implementation
Core Engine: Advanced Excel formulas and dynamic data modeling.

Automation: VBA macros implemented to automate dashboard refreshing, scenario toggling, and PDF reporting.

Visualization: Integrated charts for real-time monitoring of budget health and risk exposure.

▶️ Usage Instructions
Inputs: Adjust the parameters in the Scenario Summary or data input sheets to reflect current estimates.

Refresh: Click the "Refresh Dashboard" button to ensure all formulas and Pivot Tables are updated.

Scenario Selection: Utilize the integrated scenario buttons to instantly calculate the impact of different operational assumptions.

Export: Use the "Export to PDF" feature to generate a professional report for stakeholder review.
